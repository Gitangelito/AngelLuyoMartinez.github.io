Project: Analyzing Consumer Behavior in the Fashion Industry Using SQL

In this project, I explored a dataset that captures purchasing behavior across various demographics. The dataset includes detailed information on the number of males and females, categorized by age, who purchased clothing items. My analysis focused on understanding consumer patterns across different age groups: 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55-64, and 65+.

Using SQL, I segmented the data to explore key metrics such as the categories of items purchased, the total number of transactions, and the payment methods used by each demographic. Additionally, I analyzed the impact of promotions and discounts on purchasing behavior and how subscription status influenced customer decisions.

This comprehensive analysis provides insights into how different age groups engage with fashion retail, highlighting trends, preferences, and the effectiveness of marketing strategies.
